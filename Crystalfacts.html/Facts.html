Amethyst

  1. 🔮Amethyst is a beautiful purple variety of quartz, prized for its calming energy and striking color.
  2. 🪨Spiritually, it’s often seen as a stone of peace, protection, and clarity.
  3. 🧠Many believe amethyst helps quiet the mind, enhance intuition, and connect with higher states of consciousness.
  4. 🩹It’s thought to promote emotional balance, relieve stress, and offer spiritual protection, making it a popular choice for meditation and healing rituals throughout history.
